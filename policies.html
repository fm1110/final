Shopping Policies

Financing

  • Installment Plans
    • Customers can opt for interest-free installment payment options through partnered financial services for orders above a certain amount.
    • Flexible repayment periods ranging from 3 to 12 months.
  • Sustainable Purchase Discounts
    • Exclusive discounts for bulk purchases, particularly for businesses or events promoting sustainability and modest fashion.
  • Partnership and Wholesale Financing
    • Tailored financing options for boutique retailers and collaborators looking to stock Malkan Modesty products.
  • Eco-Credit Program
    • Loyal customers earn eco-credits for every purchase, which can be redeemed on future orders, promoting sustainable shopping habits.

Delivery

  • Processing Time
    • Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after confirmation.
    • Custom or made-to-order items may require additional time, typically 7-10 business days.
  • Shipping Options
    • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days.
    • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2-4 business days for an additional fee.
    • Free Shipping: Available for orders exceeding a specified amount, depending on the region.
  • International Shipping
    • We ship globally, with delivery times varying based on destination. Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the customer's responsibility.
  • Sustainability Focus
    • Orders are packaged in eco-friendly, recyclable materials to align with our commitment to sustainability.

Returns & Refunds

  • Hassle-free refund policy within 14 days for unworn and undamaged products with original packaging.
  • Store credit available for returns beyond 14 days.
  • If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us immediately. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

Our History

Malkan Modesty was founded in 2015 by visionary entrepreneur Aisha Malkan, who sought to merge sustainability with timeless, modest fashion. The company started as a small home-based business in Dubai, crafting elegant abayas from recycled fabrics sourced locally. Aisha's mission was to create environmentally conscious garments without compromising on style or cultural values.

In its early days, Malkan Modesty offered a limited collection of abayas in neutral tones, each piece uniquely crafted with intricate embroidery and modern silhouettes. These designs quickly gained attention for their eco-friendly ethos and refined elegance.

As demand grew, the company expanded its operations, establishing partnerships with sustainable fabric suppliers and investing in innovative recycling techniques. By 2020, Malkan Modesty had launched an international e-commerce platform, allowing customers worldwide to embrace sustainable modest fashion. The brand diversified its offerings to include hijabs, tunics, and kaftans, all crafted with the same dedication to sustainability and quality.

Today, Malkan Modesty is a global leader in eco-conscious modest fashion, with flagship stores in major cities, collaborations with ethical designers, and a loyal customer base. The brand continues to champion sustainability, donating a portion of its profits to environmental causes and hosting workshops on sustainable fashion practices. Malkan Modesty remains a trailblazer in proving that modesty and sustainability can go hand in hand.

Why use us?

At Malkan Modesty, we stand out by offering exquisitely designed abayas crafted from recycled fabrics, blending timeless elegance with a commitment to sustainability. Our dedication to ethical practices, exceptional craftsmanship, and personalized customer care ensures that every piece is both beautiful and meaningful. By choosing us, you support a brand that values cultural authenticity, environmental responsibility, and empowering women worldwide.
